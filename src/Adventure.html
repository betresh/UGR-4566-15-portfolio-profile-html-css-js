TRAVEL STORIES

SEMIEN MOUNTAINS

Visiting the Semien Mountains was an experience that challenged me and helped me grow. The tough hikes taught me to keep going even when things got hard, and the beautiful views and wildlife sparked my curiosity and creativity. Camping under the stars and climbing Ras Dashen showed me the value of staying focused and pushing through challenges. This trip matched my love for adventure and reminded me that I can handle new challenges with a positive attitude.

MEDANIEALEM GEDAM

Visiting this spirtual place was a special and peaceful experience. Walking through the ancient rock-hewn churches, I felt connected to history and faith. The sound of priests chanting and the quiet, sacred atmosphere left a deep impression on me. Watching pilgrims pray with such devotion made me think about the power of belief and the beauty of spirituality. This trip helped me appreciate Ethiopian heritage and gave me a chance to reflect and feel grateful
